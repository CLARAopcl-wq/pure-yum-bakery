About Us

Meet Sam & Kristin

Kristin30 years baking
SamItalian baking roots

We are newly family — Sam married Kristin's son — and both realized how much we love to bake! Since we are our family's go-to for anything sweet, salty, or savory, it made sense to join our talents and love of amazing flavors.

Kristin has been making cakes and desserts for nearly every family birthday for the past 30 years. Sam grew up in an Italian family that loves to bake and has a strong palate for flavor. Together we make something special.

Woman-owned. Family-operated. Central Ohio proud.
